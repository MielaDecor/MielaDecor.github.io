About Miela Decor

Miela is a place to sell simple products for room decoration, helping you to unleash your creativity. Founded in 2022 as a result of a course of four young people, the name Miela means lovely in Litva. Miela also means that by putting a compact product in the room, you can change the vibe, which can make a huge difference to your day

“Spaces with a touch of difference”
Inspired by the little things, Miela wants to help spark creativity, embellishing every corner so that every home is unique
